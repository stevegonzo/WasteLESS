Calories: 475                     Cuisine: Mediterranean

INGREDIENTS


1 tablespoon garlic powder

1 tablespoon dried basil

1/2 teaspoon salt

4 (6 oz) salmons

2 tablespoons butter

4 lemon wedges




STEPS

1. Stir together the garlic powder, basil, and salt in a small bowl; rub in equal amounts onto the salmon fillets.

2..Melt butter over skillet over medium heat

3. Cook salmon in the butter until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side

4. Serve each piece of salmon with a lemon edge

5. Enjoy!

Calories: 550              Cuisine: American/German

INGREDIENTS


2 cups (~1 small) red potato

1 large head of broccoli (~ 1 and 1/2 cups)

9 ounces smoked sausage

6 tablespoons olive oil

1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

1 tablespoon dried oregano

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon pepper


STEPS

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

2.. Line a large sheet pan with foil or parchment paper.

3. Prep the veggies: chop the red potatoes (pretty small pieces here so they will be tender in time), chop the broccoli, and coin the sausage in thick slices.

4. Place all the veggies and sausage on a sheet pan. Pour the olive oil and all the spices on top. Toss to evenly coat all the veggies and meat. (I use this 15x21 inch sheet pan.

5.Bake 15 minutes, remove from the oven and flip/stir all the veggies around. Return to the oven and bake for another 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are crisp tender and sausage is browned.

6. Enjoy with rice or quinoa and fresh parsley if desired.

Calories: 450                      Cuisine: American

INGREDIENTS

6 cups of mixed salad green

1 ounce of Italian dressing mix

2 plum (Roma) tomatoes, chopped

1 tablespoons of olive oil

2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar


STEPS


1. In medium bowl, mix vinegars and water. Stir in dressing mix. Stir in oil. Divide dressing mixture in half.

2.. Place chicken in shallow glass or plastic dish or heavy-duty resealable plastic food-storage bag. Pour half of the dressing mixture over chicken; turn chicken to coat. Cover dish or seal bag and refrigerate 15 minutes. Cover and refrigerate remaining dressing mixture

3. Heat coals or gas grill for direct heat. Remove chicken from marinade; reserve marinade. Cover and grill chicken over medium heat 15 to 20 minutes, turning and brushing with marinade occasionally, until juice of chicken is clear when center of thickest part is cut (170°F). Discard any remaining marinade.

4. Cut chicken into slices. Serve chicken on salad greens with remaining dressing mixture. Top with tomatoes.

Calories: 650                               Cuisine: American

INGREDIENTS

2 Eggs

2 Cooked Bacon Strips

8 ounces of Orange Juice

2 Pieces of Wheat Bread (Toast)


STEPS


1. PLACE eggs in a saucepan large enough to hold them in a single layer

2. PLACE Wheat Bread in a Microwave Oven/Toaster

2. ADD cold water to cover the eggs by 1 inch

3. HEAT over high heat just to boiling

4. REMOVE from burner and COVER pan

5. Let eggs stand in hot pan for about 12 minutes for large eggs (9 minutes for medium eggs; 15 minutes for extra-large eggs.) DRAIN

6. SHOCK the eggs in a bowl of ice water to cool them immediately.

7. REMOVE toast from toaster

8. POUR Orange Juice in a glass

9. Enjoy!
