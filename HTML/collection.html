View Your Food Collection

Press the Food Items button to view all

of the items currently in your collection

While viewing your Food Collection, press

the Add New Item Button

When prompted, enter the details about the

item you are adding

The name of the item

The date you want to use the item by

The quantity of the item you are adding

While viewing your Food Collection, press

the Used button next to the item you used

and no longer wish to track

This will remove the item from your Food

Collection

While viewing your Food Collection, press

the Trash button next to the item you threw

away

This will remove the item from your Food

Collection and add it to your Waste

Collection

View Your Waste Collection

Press the Waste Items button to view all

of the items currently in your collection

While viewing your Waste Collection, press

the Add New Item Button

When prompted, enter the details about the

item you are adding

The name of the item

The date you threw the item away

The quantity of the item you threw away

While viewing your Waste Collection, press

the Remove button next to the item you

would like to remove from the collection
